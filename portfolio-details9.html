Project Description

The study examines paddy cultivation in Sri Lanka, revealing variations in yields due to climate, soil fertility, and agricultural practices. Climate change is a major driver, necessitating climate-smart practices and resilient infrastructure. Sustainable farming methods, conservation agriculture, and farmer training programs are crucial for long-term viability. The research contributes to agricultural science, climate resilience, and sustainable development.
